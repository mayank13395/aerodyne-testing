Why Aerodyne India?

Aerodyne India is a DT³ (Drone Tech, Data Tech, and Digital Transformation) solutions provider of drone-bas ed enterprise solutions, and a pioneer in the use of AI as an enabling technology for large-scale data operations, analytics and process optimization.

Aerodyne India offers turnkey enterprise solutions for location intelligence, surveying and mapping, inspection, emergency response, precision agriculture, and comprehensive asset management across a multitude of industries, including oil and gas, renewables, power, mining, and infrastructure. Our bespoke service model enables our clients to achieve cost-effective aerial capabilities swiftly and quickly.

  • Advanced Automation and Analytics
  • User-Friendly Process Optimization Interface
  • Complete Regulatory Compliance
  • Immediate Deployment of Drones
  • No Capital Expenditure
  • No Training Cost
  • No Maintenance Cost
  • Protection Against Obsolescence

Applications

Geospatial intelligence
Aerodyne India’s survey-grade drones provide a cost-effective platform to gather CAD- and GIS-ready spatial data quickly and accurately. We also extract actionable insights from large datasets to allow you to make data-backed decisions
Emergency response
Having a quick, strategic aerial overview during emergency situations enables better planning and execution. Aerodyne India is a pioneer in offering an integrated aerial support system to first responders
Precision agriculture
Precision farming is one of the biggest benefactors of drone data services. From mapping and estimating tree count to crop spraying and more, we are here to assist you get immediate returns on your investment

Security and surveillance
High vantage coupled with speed, agility and endurance gives drones the edge in uplifting the effectiveness of security and surveillance activities
Oil and gas
Reduce cost and risk, even as you witness unprecedented gains in efficiency by using Aerodyne India’s best drone services
Natural resources
By providing a detailed and accurate overview of large, hard-to-access areas, drones play a pivotal role in managing any nation’s natural resources such as forest reserves, river systems, sands, and fertile land masses
Environmental management
Geotagged high-resolution live video and images allow for a quick and effective method for monitoring the environment. On top of that, a multitude of sensors can be installed for data sampling
Project monitoring and reporting
Our project management and reporting tool, myPRISM, is being used in many iconic development projects around the world. This proven and matured service solution gives our customers immediate returns on their investment
Engineering and survey
Compared to traditional methods, Aerodyne India can capture and process survey grade 2D and 3D data at an unprecedented speed and accuracy
Mining
Eradicate risk to personnel from carrying out survey works in dangerous environments. Add speed and accuracy (volumetric analysis, inspection of mine walls and slopes), and you have an unparalleled platform to get the data you need
